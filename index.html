How It Works

Step 1: Join a Tutoring Session

Choose between small group or one-to-one lessons. Students on Free School Meals are eligible for group sessions.

Step 2: Complete H5P Homework

After each lesson, you’ll receive interactive H5P tasks and a downloadable PDF version for offline access.

Step 3: Earn Your Discount

Hand in your homework before the next session to qualify for the lower fee (£15 group / £20 solo).
